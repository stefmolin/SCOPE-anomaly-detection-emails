NOTES + GUIDELINES:

This is NOT your ‘Dashboard’
• This alert is not to be used in place of your tableau dashboards (like the ‘Americas Dashboard’ or ‘MD Dashboard’).
• However, this alert can help you interpret the trend you see in these dashboards. (Example: If you see something that appears concerning in a dashboard, but you do not receive a SCOPE alert, it is unlikely that there is any real issue.)

This is NOT a Business Escalation alert
• SCOPE aims to detect business anomalies using statistics.
• It relies on daily aggregated data and wouldn’t necessarily detect short term issues captured by Business Escalation.

The Model
• SCOPE uses a Chi-square test to detect anomalies.
• This model uses the 30 days preceding today to determine anomalism and trigger the alert.

SCOPE Alerts
• Alerts can be for positive or negative anomalies.
• Alerts are for yesterday (the day prior to the date of this email).
• This is for Tier 1 only.
• RexT includes Display, In-App, and DPA, but excludes Email.
• RexT is for Local + Imports. There is no bridge applied.
• All RexT values are in $USD.
• The $USD constant currency conversion rate is used.

Regional Account Alerts Highlights
• The regional Account Alerts are supplied to give more insight into the possible drivers of the regional performance.
• These sections highlight the ‘top’ and ‘bottom’ five account-level alerts that were triggered for each region. “Top” means that the alert occurred on an account with yesterday's RexT higher than the average of the last 7 days. “Bottom” means that the alert occurred on an account with yesterday's RexT higher than the average of the last 7 days. The difference is the metric used for selecting the account alerts to be highlighted.
